01 / Shipping & Returns

Shipping & Returns

Shipping

  • Origin — All pieces ship from Barcelona, Spain.
  • Dispatch — Orders from Drop 001 are dispatched within 72 hours of the drop closing.
  • Spain — Tracked shipping, 2–4 business days.
  • European Union — Tracked shipping, 4–7 business days.
  • Rest of the world — Not available during Drop 001.

Returns

  • Withdrawal — You have 14 calendar days from delivery to return any piece, in line with EU consumer law. The piece must be unworn, unwashed and in its original packaging.
  • Size exchanges — Subject to remaining stock. Drop 001 is 75 pieces with no restock — check the size guide carefully before ordering.
  • Refunds — Processed within 14 days of receiving the returned piece, to the original payment method.
  • How — Contact us via Instagram @ggsilence with your order number.

03 / Privacy

Privacy Policy

Silence extends to your data. We collect the absolute minimum.

What we collect

  • Waitlist — Name, email address and size preference, only when you submit the form.
  • Nothing else — No advertising trackers, no analytics profiles sold to anyone, no third-party pixels.

Why

  • Purpose — To notify you about Drop 001 access and, if you bought a piece, to fulfil your order.
  • Legal basis — Your consent (waitlist) and contract performance (orders), under the GDPR / RGPD.

Where it lives

  • Processing — Form submissions are processed by Netlify (our hosting provider) and stored securely. Emails are sent only by us.
  • No sharing — Your data is never sold or shared with third parties beyond the providers needed to run this site and deliver orders.

Your rights

You can request access, correction or deletion of your data at any time via Instagram @ggsilence. One message is enough — we delete, we confirm, we stay silent.
